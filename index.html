We're complicated creatures

When we travel, we're trying to discover the essence of a region during a moment in time. But what happens when there are stories.

Women: We're complicated creatures. One day we're throwing on thigh-high Giuseppe boots and a disco minidress for a night of total mayhem, and the next, we're tying on an apron and prepping muffin batter. Party girl to aspiring pastry chef within a matter of hours? Because, why not! For centuries such a mercurial state of mind (and dress) was considered a weakness of the fairer sex, but these days the woman "who can do both" is a reminder to all men that women can hardly be pigeonholed. In fact, female shape-shifters who morph from tomboy to total glamazon with a quick outfit change happen.
